The following rules have been put into place to protect you.
We highly encourage you to read and memorise them as you may be questioned about the rules upon entry


  • NO CONSENT, NO PLAY

  • NO TOUCHING, GRABBING, WHIPPING, SPANKING, CHOKING OR ANYTHING OF THE SORT UNLESS YOU HAVE CLEAR PERMISSION

  • NO BLOOD PLAY. THIS INCLUDES VAMPIRE GLOVES & PADDLES. IF YOU DRAW BLOOD, STOP.

  • NO WAX PLAY

  • NO NEEDLE PLAY

  • ALL WATERSPORTS MUST BE CONTAINED WITHIN 1 METRE OF TOILET

  • ALL GUESTS MUST SANITISE EQUIPMENT & FURNITURE, BEFORE & AFTER USE. USE SANITISATION STATIONS PROVIDED

  • NO EXTREME TOXICATION. KNOW YOUR LIMITS. YOU WITHDRAW YOUR ABILITY TO GIVE AND ASSUME CONSENT

  • WHEN UNDER THE INFLUENCE OF DRUGS/EXESSIVE ALCOHOL CONSUMPTION
